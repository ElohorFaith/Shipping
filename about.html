About Us

GROUP OVERVIEW

The history of the Daddo Group dates back to 1981, when its founder and chairman,
Alhaji Bamanga Tukur, embraced the challenge of pioneering indigenous provision of professional
Ship and Tanker Agency Services in Nigeria, by incorporating Daddo Maritime Services Limited.

Daddo Maritime Services Limited has since established itself as the leading Tanker Agency Services
provider in the country, with a solid reputation for efficiency, safety and professionalism, a “trade mark”
service quality, the demand for which has prompted its expansion into other countries on the continent and beyond.

Subsequent vertical & horizontal expansion, the incorporation of other companies and the consolidation into a group,
can be explained in terms of a combination in varying proportions of the following factors;

  • The need to develop and streamline value added services into business units;
  • The quest for efficiency to meet and surpass the expectations of customers;
  • The logical business sense to tap into and harness synergies and comparative advantages;
  • The zeal to develop capacity and generate employment locally yet preventing capital flight.
  • The ambition to ensure commensurate returns to shareholders;
