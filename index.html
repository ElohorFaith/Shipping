Chairman's Message

It is over two decades ago that we challenged ourselves to pioneering the establishment of an indigenous “world class” tanker agency
services company in Nigeria.
We have succeeded in responding to market demands in this regard and have since further challenged ourselves to maintaining
leadership position, by offering services comparable if not better than what is obtained anywhere in the world.

Our measure of success in this direction is not limited to our dominant market share position, but most importantly on client
satisfaction levels. Our quest for excellence in this continuous endeavor has become our trade mark.With the right tools,
people and attitude, we are continuously improving and expanding on the range, the scope and depth of services we
make available to our clients, without compromising on the fundamental requirements of quality, safety and timeliness.

We are proud and delighted to welcome you to the “Daddo” experience of competence, versatility and dedication in service delivery.
